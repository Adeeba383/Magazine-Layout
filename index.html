THE URBAN FASHION
REVOLUTION

Streetwear is more than just a style. It's a cultural phenomenon that has transformed the fashion landscape. From humble beginnings on the street of urban neighborhoods to the runways of high-end fashion shows, streetwear has become a global symbol of self-expression, authenticity, and youth culture. An outfit is considered streetwear when it combines casual, comfortable pieces with bold, urban elements. Key components include oversized clothing like hoodies and tees, statement sneakers or boots, and accessories such as caps or chains. The fashion industry defines streetwear as fashionable, casual clothing worn by followers of popular culture. The majority of these followers are under 30, live in urban areas, and belong to a subculture group (ex. skateboarders or hip-hop music fans).
